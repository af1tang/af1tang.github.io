### Voigt-McKampf Test ###

Inspired by Voight-Kampff test from Blade Runner, the Voigt-McKampf test is designed to distinguish chatbots from humans using conversations. 
In this demo, you will be asked to have a short conversation with a *personalized open-domain conversational model* -- PersonaGPT. 
For Experiment 1, the personality traits of the bot is given, and you can score its performance (i.e., how well it "stays in character"). 
By contrast, Experiment 2 does not give you the personality traits, and you will instead make an educated guess as to what set of personality traits characterize your partner. 

When you create an account, you will be asked to sign a consent form (since this is an IRB-approved experiment). By consenting, your conversational data with the chatbots will be used to build a publicly available database.
